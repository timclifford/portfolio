Secure and scalable, content managed, fully responsive community website.

I chose Drupal because it is cost effective and proven in the private sector as a secure and scalable open source content management system. Friends Life required a CMS that non-technical content administrators could easily use with the ability to manage content and diverse functionality at an enterprise scale. They recognized that Drupal had the unique capability to provide the power, flexibility and security, while still providing unique modular functionality that was required to build their community project.

Mobile view

Objectives

In order to complement such a large community based website, Friends Life needed a sharp, easy-to-maintain and fully responsive website. The site was required to provide employees with a wealth of financial tools and resources, join webinars and collaborate with other community members. I sought to achieve these results by incorporating the following features in their site:

  • Community/social group functionality
  • A webinar center for all employees to take part in
  • Financial resources and tools
  • The ability to moderate comments being posted in group and webinar discussions
  • Created a re-brandable theme for moderators
  • Custom built registration and group functionality
